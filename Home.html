HCIPAK 2025

The 1st International Summer School on Human-Computer Interaction

Rachna College of Engineering & Technology,
Gujranwala, Pakistan

April 28-30, 2025

The 1st International Summer School on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI 2025) is designed to provide an immersive learning experience for students interested in the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

The event will feature a series of lectures, hands-on workshops, and interactive discussions, covering key topics such as user experience (UX) design, usability testing, interaction design, accessibility, artificial intelligence in HCI, and emerging trends in human-centered computing. Students will gain both theoretical insights and practical skills, enabling them to conduct impactful research and develop innovative applications in HCI.

This year, HCI 2025 will offer participants the opportunity to network with experts, collaborate with peers, and explore real-world applications of HCI in various domains such as healthcare, education, virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and smart systems.

Furthermore, students attending HCI 2025 will be encouraged to submit research papers, project ideas, and case studies to various academic tracks and workshops related to HCI, UX design, and interactive technologies. Selected students will also have the chance to showcase their work and receive valuable feedback from international experts.

Venue and Accommodation

The Flattees Hotel in Gujranwala is a premium accommodation choice, offering top-tier hospitality and modern amenities. Strategically located in the heart of Gujranwala, it provides easy access to business districts, shopping areas, and cultural landmarks. The hotel offers a range of well-furnished rooms, an in-house restaurant, and conference facilities, making it ideal for business travelers and tourists alike.

The 2025 Summer School sessions will take place at a designated conference hall within Flattees Hotel, ensuring a comfortable and professional environment for all attendees. Additionally, participants staying at the hotel can enjoy seamless access to event activities, networking sessions, and evening social gatherings.

How to Reach Flattees Hotel, Gujranwala

Flattees Hotel is easily accessible from major transport hubs. The closest airports are Allama Iqbal International Airport, Lahore (LHE), approximately 80 km away, and Sialkot International Airport (SKT), around 50 km away. The city is well connected by road and rail, making travel convenient for both domestic and international visitors.

From Allama Iqbal International Airport (LHE), Lahore

By Car: The most convenient option is to take a private taxi or ride-hailing service (Uber, Careem), which takes around 1.5 to 2 hours depending on traffic.

By Bus: Various intercity bus services operate between Lahore and Gujranwala, departing from the Lahore Bus Terminal. The journey takes approximately 1.5 hours, and tickets can be booked via Daewoo Express, Skyways, or Faisal Movers.

By Train: Travelers can take a Pakistan Railways train from Lahore Railway Station to Gujranwala Railway Station, which is just a 10-minute drive from Flattees Hotel.

From Sialkot International Airport (SKT)

By Car: A direct taxi or private ride-hailing service is the fastest option, taking 45 minutes to 1 hour.

By Bus: Several local and intercity bus services operate between Sialkot and Gujranwala, with a travel time of approximately 1 hour.

By Train: Pakistan Railways operates trains between Sialkot and Gujranwala, providing a cost-effective travel option.
